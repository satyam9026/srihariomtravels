POKHARA TOUR

Pokhara Tour and Travel from Gorakhpur – A Journey to the City of Lakes

If you want a peaceful but thrilling vacation destination, Pokhara Tour and Travel from Gorakhpur is one of the best options. Pokhara, known as the “City of Lakes”, is one of the most beautiful and relaxing cities in Nepal.

Mountain ranges, waterfalls, and calm lakes surround it, making Pokhara an ideal retreat for nature lovers, families, and thrill seekers.

Why Visit Pokhara?

Pokhara is well known for its tranquil atmosphere, natural beauty, and views of the Himalayas. The city lies at the base of the Annapurna Range, and on a clear day, snow-covered peaks such as Machhapuchhre (Fishtail Mountain) can be seen above the clouds.

Leave the bustle of the cities behind and enjoy the peaceful and refreshing environment of Pokhara — ideal for those who need a break from busy life. Whether you are a sightseer, thrill seeker, or spiritual traveler, Pokhara offers a unique charm.

Journey from Gorakhpur to Pokhara

The driving distance from Gorakhpur to Pokhara is around 280 kilometers and it takes approximately 8 to 9 hours. The journey begins from Gorakhpur and passes through the Sunauli border into Nepal.

Along the way, you can enjoy scenic views of hills, rivers, and small villages that make the road trip memorable.

Many tour and travel companies in Gorakhpur offer cars, tempo travelers, and buses for Pokhara tours. Private tours as well as group packages are available based on your travel style and budget.

Top Attractions in Pokhara

  • Phewa Lake: Pokhara’s center, where you can enjoy a peaceful boat ride and see mountains reflected in the lake.
  • Davis Falls: A stunning underground waterfall — one of the most unique attractions in Pokhara.

More Attractions in Pokhara

  • Gupteshwar Mahadev Cave: The cave near Davis Falls contains a sacred temple of Lord Shiva.
  • World Peace Pagoda: A white stupa on a hilltop offering panoramic views of Pokhara city and Phewa Lake.
  • Sarangkot View Point: Perfect for sunrise and paragliding — you can see the entire Pokhara valley and the Himalayan range from here.

Best Time to Visit

The best season for Pokhara tour from Gorakhpur is from March to June and September to November. During these months, the temperature is comfortable and the sky remains clear.

This makes it perfect for sightseeing, boating, paragliding, and other adventure activities.

Tour Package Highlights

Most Gorakhpur to Pokhara tour packages include:

  • Pickup and drop from Gorakhpur
  • All transfers and sightseeing in Nepal
  • Hotel accommodation with breakfast
  • Border assistance and guide support
  • Optional adventure activities like paragliding or boating

You can also combine Pokhara with nearby destinations like Kathmandu, Lumbini, or Muktinath for a complete Nepal experience.

Final Thoughts

Pokhara Tour and Travel from Gorakhpur is a perfect mix of relaxation, natural beauty, and adventure. Whether you want peace by the lakes, thrilling mountain views, or spiritual calm, Pokhara delivers an unforgettable travel experience.

Pokhara Tour From Gorakhpur is the best blend of peace, adventure, and nature. Located only a couple of hours’ drive from the Indian border, Pokhara provides an “abroad” travel experience with no flights needed and no visa required.

Whether you want to relax by a lake, feel blessed at temples, or participate in heart-stopping adventures, Pokhara has it all.

So plan your next journey from Gorakhpur to Pokhara — and experience the allure of Nepal’s most picturesque city, where every mountain, waterfall, and lake narrates a lovely tale.
