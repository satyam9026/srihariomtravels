All Nepal Tour Itinerary (8 Nights / 9 Days)
Day Destination Activities
Day 1 Gorakhpur to Chitwan Travel through scenic landscapes to Chitwan National Park for a night stay.
Day 2 Chitwan to Janakpur Visit Janki Palace, an important pilgrimage site, before settling in Janakpur.
Day 3 Janakpur to Kathmandu Journey to the capital city, Kathmandu, known for its rich culture and heritage.
Day 4 Kathmandu Local sightseeing including Swayambhunath and Pashupatinath Temple.
Day 5 Kathmandu to Pokhara Travel via Manokamna, with the option to take a cable car to the temple.
Day 6 Pokhara to Muktinath Optional flight or road trip to Muktinath, a sacred site for Hindus and Buddhists.
Day 7 Jomsom to Pokhara Return to Pokhara, enjoying the beautiful scenery along the way.
Day 8 Pokhara to Lumbini Travel to Lumbini, the birthplace of Gautam Buddha for an overnight stay.
Day 9 Lumbini to Gorakhpur Darshan at Buddha's birthplace before driving back to Gorakhpur.
