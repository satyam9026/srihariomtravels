VARANASI TOUR

Varanasi Tour Package from Gorakhpur – A Peaceful and Cultural Trip

Varanasi (Kashi / Banaras) is a very old city and a spiritual city of India. Varanasi is a place quite busy with travellers from all over the world, full of energy of Ganga river and visiting old temples and traditional Indian culture.

If you are living in Gorakhpur or surrounding areas, then Varanasi makes an excellent short trip destination for you. Because it isn’t a great distance, and the travel was easy.

Many travelers from Gorakhpur choose this tour package because they get a complete experience in just 1 or 2 days. You can see holy temples, historic places, and beautiful ghats of river Ganga without any stress.

This tour is also good for senior citizens, families and first-time tourists.

Why Choose This Trip from Gorakhpur?

Gorakhpur to Varanasi distance is approximately 225 to 240 km (route may vary). So this journey becomes very easy by car / traveler / tempo traveler.

Most travel companies provide door to door pickup and drop service. So you do not need to worry about long travel planning. You simply sit in the vehicle and enjoy the trip.

This is also a budget friendly tour. You get a proper spiritual and cultural experience in a small budget.

Places You Will Visit in This Tour

1) Kashi Vishwanath Temple

This is the main temple of Varanasi. Darshan here is a very powerful experience. Many people feel mental peace after visiting here.

2) Dashashwamedh Ghat

In the evening, you will watch the famous Ganga Aarti. This is the highlight of this trip. The lights, mantras and atmosphere are magical.

3) Assi Ghat

This place is popular with youth and tourists. You can click photos and enjoy the riverside atmosphere.

4) Sarnath

This is a very important Buddhist place. Lord Buddha gave his first teaching here. It is very peaceful and calming.

5) Local Banaras Market

You can do shopping of real Banarasi sarees, silk fabrics, and also taste famous Banaras street food.

What Is Usually Included in a Package?

  • Pickup from Gorakhpur
  • AC vehicle transport
  • Hotel stay in Varanasi (standard / deluxe)
  • Sightseeing of temples and ghats
  • Ganga Aarti experience
  • Drop back to Gorakhpur

Some packages also include boat ride and VIP darshan (extra cost).

Best Time to Travel

You can visit Varanasi anytime. But October to March is the best weather for comfortable walking and sightseeing.

Final Conclusion

Varanasi Tour Package from Gorakhpur is a perfect blend of spirituality, comfort and culture. The journey is brief, the immersion is intense, and the memories last a lifetime.

In one short trip you can visit heritage temples, world famous ghats, river Ganga, Sarnath history and local market culture.

This trip is perfect for families, students, couples and elderly. If you want a hassle-free and meaningful holiday near Gorakhpur, then Varanasi is one of the best choices you can select.
