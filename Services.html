Pashupati Nath Mandir

Pashupatinath Temple, located in Kathmandu, Nepal, is one of the most sacred Hindu temples in the world. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it is a major pilgrimage site and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The temple complex is situated on the banks of the Bagmati River and is known for its stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and vibrant festivals.
