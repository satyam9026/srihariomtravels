Lucknow, a capital of Uttar Pradesh, is known as the City of Nawabs for its royal background in history, culture, hospitality and mouthwatering food. If you are located in Gorakhpur and are looking for a brief holiday trip featuring a modern city experience and a glimpse of heritage, the Lucknow Tour Package from Gorakhpur is an ideal tour for you. This trip allows you to rest, entertain and create wonderful memories with your own.

Why is Lucknow trip very easy from Gorakhpur?

Gorakhpur to Lucknow distance is approximately 260 to 270 km. You can travel by AC car, tempo traveler or train. Many travel companies provide direct pickup and drop service. So, you don‚Äôt need to worry about transport or route. The travel is smooth and comfortable.

Best Places Covered in This Package

1) Bara Imambara

This is the most famous monument in Lucknow. The architecture is grand and amazing. The Bhool Bhulaiya maze inside is very unique and adventurous.

2) Chota Imambara

This place is beautiful, peaceful and perfect for photography. It shows the royal art of Awadh culture.

3) Rumi Darwaza

It is one of the main landmarks of Lucknow. People take pictures here because it looks like a giant gateway.

4) Ambedkar Park / Janeshwar Mishra Park

These modern parks are very big and well-maintained. Good for family walks, relaxing and evening photos.

5) Gomti Riverfront

A beautiful riverside area with lighting at night. This is a good place to see the modern side of Lucknow.

6) Hazratganj Market

Perfect market for shopping perfumes, chikan clothes, bags and gifts.

Food Experience in Lucknow

Lucknow food is world famous.

In this tour you can taste:

  • Tunday Kabab
  • Galouti Kabab
  • Lucknow Biryani
  • Malai Paan
  • Basket Chaat
  • Sheermal

This food culture makes the trip more memorable.
